About Twelve Scents

Filling the empty shelf with heroes who look like our children.

We're a Black-owned, faith-rooted children's publisher creating Bible stories that center brown children — so every little reader can see themselves in scripture.

Our story

It started at bedtime. Searching for Bible storybooks for our own children, we kept asking the same question — where are the heroes who look like them? The shelves were beautiful, but the brown faces were missing.

So we began writing. Twelve Scents Publishing creates inspirational stories that are faithful to scripture, rooted in biblical heritage, and overflowing with the dignity and joy of brown children.

Today our books are read at bedtimes, in classrooms, and in children's ministries across the country — and we're just getting started.

Our mission

"To put a Bible hero who looks like them into the hands of every brown child — building courage, character, and belonging through stories worth reading again and again."
